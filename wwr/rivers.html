Route One

Route One is our shorter more mild route. It takes on average 90 minutes to complete only being 3 miles long. The river is fairly slow averaging around 2 miles per hour with very mild rapids. This route is perfect for families with young children.

$20.00 per person

Route Two

Route Two is our longer route with more intense rapid. It takes on average 2 hours to complete and it is 9 miles long. The average speed being 4.5 miles per hour. It is our most popular route and has very beautiful scenery.

$25.00 per person

Campsite

Our campsite is only a three minute walk from the river. It has bathrooms and showers for anyone who needs it, and there are campfires at each individual campsite. There is also a playground and a volleyball court too. Our staff prides itself in being easy to get ahold of in case of an emergency

$60.00 a night
