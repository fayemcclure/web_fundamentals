Purpose

For this assignment, I changed my mind for my old idea becauseI realized it was going to be hard for me to create a website that looks good and is organized. Instead I am doing a website for almost a campsite, but people rent out treehouses to stay in. I choose to do this for my assignment because I know I will be able to make it look good and it’s something that I will enjoy doing. I was able to find some pictures I really liked to use for this assignment. The homepage is just going to be for advertisement, I will write a paragraph saying how important customer satisfaction is to my business and have a few five star reviews on my page. For the second page I am going to show my different treehouses that I am going to be renting out complete with a description and how much it costs to rent per night. On the final page I am going to write about what other benefits come from renting our treehouses, I’m going to say that there is a lake and hiking trails nearby; each treehouse has a fireplace, free wifi, heating, and plumbing; and I am also going to say that there is a hot tub.

Why my site?

I love being outdoors and being surrounded by nature, however I don’t really like camping that much because I like being able to shower and stay clean. I decided to make a website for a place you can rent out a treehouse for a couple of nights at a place in the middle of the woods that’s almost like a campsite, but you still have a shower, heat, and a kitchen. I think it would be fun to rent out a treehouse for a few nights with my friends and I am sure many other people would think so too. Especially with people like me who like the outdoors but aren’t the biggest fan of camping.

What to Expect

My site is going to have the home page be advertising my fake business and show some good reviews and also a paragraph on how my business prioritizes customer satisfaction. The second page is going to show pictures of the different treehouses and how much it costs a night for each one and a brief description of each treehouse. The last page is going to show what all is included when you stay at the treehouses.
