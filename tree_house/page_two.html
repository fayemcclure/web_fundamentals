Relax in Nature

Make dreams come true

Sunrise Lake 100 feet from the Resort

Take a serene stroll along the Sunset Ridge Trail, conveniently located within walking distance from our treehouse retreat. This gentle 2-mile loop trail offers enchanting forest views and stunning sunset panoramas, perfect for an evening hike or a morning stroll.

Each of our cozy treehouses is equipped with modern amenities to ensure a comfortable and memorable stay. Relax by the fireplace on chilly evenings, stay warm with efficient heating systems, and enjoy the convenience of indoor plumbing. Additionally, stay connected with complimentary WiFi access throughout your stay. Our aim is to provide a rustic yet luxurious experience amidst the tranquility of nature.

Unwind and indulge in our soothing hot tub, nestled amidst the scenic surroundings of our treehouse retreat. Immerse yourself in relaxation as you soak away the stresses of everyday life, surrounded by the beauty of nature.
